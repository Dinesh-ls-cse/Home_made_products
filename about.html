Our Story

Discover the passion behind Home Made Products

Our Mission

At Home Made Products, we believe in the beauty of handcrafted items and the stories they tell. Our mission is to connect talented artisans with customers who appreciate the value of handmade goods.

We strive to create a marketplace that celebrates craftsmanship, supports sustainable practices, and preserves traditional techniques while embracing innovation.

Every product in our marketplace is carefully selected to ensure quality, authenticity, and ethical production. We're committed to transparency and fair trade, ensuring that artisans receive proper compensation for their work.

How It All Began

2020

The Idea

Home Made Products began as a simple idea between friends Emma and David, who shared a passion for handcrafted goods. After visiting local craft fairs and markets, they recognized the need for a dedicated platform to showcase artisanal products.

2021

Building the Community

We started by partnering with 15 local artisans, creating a small but dedicated community. Our first online marketplace launched, featuring a curated selection of handcrafted items from ceramics to textiles.

2022

Growing Together

As word spread, our community grew to over 100 artisans from across the country. We expanded our categories and introduced the Artisan Spotlight program to highlight the stories behind the products.

2023

Where We Are Today

Today, Home Made Products is a thriving marketplace with over 500 artisans and thousands of unique products. We've remained true to our core values of quality, authenticity, and community, while continuously improving the experience for both artisans and customers.
